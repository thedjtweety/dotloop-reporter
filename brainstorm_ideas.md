# Feature Brainstorm: Elevating Dotloop Reporter

Based on a deep dive into top competitors like Sisu, Brokermint, and CTE, here are high-impact feature concepts that could significantly enhance the Dotloop Reporter.

## 1. Gamification & Motivation (Inspired by Sisu)
Sisu excels at turning sales data into a competitive sport. We can adapt this to drive agent engagement.

*   **"Deal Bell" Animations**: When a new "Closed" transaction is detected in an upload, play a celebratory animation (confetti, ringing bell) on the dashboard.
*   **TV Mode**: A dedicated, high-contrast "Lobby Display" mode designed to be cast to a big screen in the office, rotating through leaderboards, recent wins, and team goals.
*   **Weighted Contests**: Allow managers to set up temporary contests (e.g., "Listing Blitz: April") where specific activities (Listings Taken) earn more points than others.

## 2. Financial Forecasting & "What If" Scenarios (Inspired by Brokermint)
Brokermint focuses heavily on the financial health of the brokerage. We can add predictive power to our reporting.

*   **Commission Projector**: A tool that calculates *projected* income for the next 30/60/90 days based on "Under Contract" dates and average fall-through rates.
*   **Agent "Take Home" Calculator**: A personal dashboard for agents to see their estimated net income after splits, franchise fees, and cap deductions.
*   **Goal Reverse-Engineering**: Enter a desired annual income (e.g., $200k), and the system calculates exactly how many appointments and contacts are needed daily based on historical conversion rates.

## 3. Business Health & Accountability (Inspired by CTE)
CTE is known for its rigorous tracking of business vitals. We can bring this level of clarity to the dashboard.

*   **The "Vital Signs" Ticker**: A scrolling ticker at the top of the app showing real-time health metrics: *Listings Taken (MoM)*, *Pending Volume*, *Avg Sale Price*.
*   **ROI Tracker**: A simple input form to log monthly marketing spend (Zillow, PPC), automatically calculating the "Cost Per Closing" and "ROI" for each lead source.
*   **Whiteboard View**: A digital recreation of the classic office whiteboard, showing all active deals in a grid that can be dragged and dropped between status columns (Active -> Pending -> Closed).
