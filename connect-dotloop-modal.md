# Connect Dotloop Modal - Feature Verification

## Screenshot Evidence

The "Connect Dotloop" button successfully opens a comprehensive modal that explains:

1. **What to Expect**:
   - One-Click Connection: Securely link your Dotloop account with OAuth 2.0
   - Automatic Sync: Your data updates nightly without manual uploads
   - Read-Only Access: We never modify your Dotloop data—only read it
   - Instant Revocation: Disconnect anytime from your Dotloop settings

2. **Security First**:
   - We never store your Dotloop password
   - All connections use industry-standard OAuth 2.0
   - You can revoke access instantly from your Dotloop account settings

3. **Coming Soon Notice**:
   - Clear messaging that this feature is in development
   - Encourages users to continue using CSV uploads in the meantime
